SUDHENA AGRO FARMS PVT LTD Started in the year 2017 by Much Experienced Team in Farming and Real Estate Sector.

  • Company Started in a vision to Create a Value for the Customer by Cultivation of the Red Sanders (PteroCorpus Santalinus). Red Sanders is Mostly found in the Areas of Chittoor, Prakasam, Nellore and Cuddapah districts of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Directors of SUDHENA AGRO are originated from this area and took up the cultivation of Red Sanders in Contractual mode to create a Value for their customers.
  • SUDHENA AGRO Has a vision to enter into various Agri Sectors like contractual farming, Organic Dairy Farming and food processing industries
